Unlock the timeless wisdom of the Bhagavad Gita with this edition of Bhagavad Gita Sri Krsnas Illuminations on the Perfection of Yoga , with Swami B.G. Narasingha s insightful commentary. This edition provides a clear translation of the original Sanskrit with concise commentary for the 21st century, making the ancient teachings of the Bhagavad Gita more accessible and easier to comprehend in our modern world. This invaluable resource is ideal for those seeking a deeper understanding of the eternal self (ātmā), the path of bhakti yoga and the true nature and identity of the Absolute Truth, Śrī Kṛṣṇa. Filled with powerful and life changing words of wisdom, the Bhagavad Gita is the most significant book in the world, surpassing all others in its depth of knowledge and enlightenment.
